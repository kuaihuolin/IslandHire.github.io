    THE RECRUITMENT PROCESS

    Island Hire employs a structured and efficient recruitment process to connect skilled candidates with the unique needs of our client companies.

    1. Meeting with client to understand the requirements

    The recruitment process begins with in-depth consultations with client companies to collecting specific hiring requirements， such as the job roles, qualifications, and desired skill sets.

    2. Talent Sourcing and Screening

    we course talents through various channels, such as our networks, job portals, and industry databases to identify potential candidates. Our recruitment team conducts the screenings of candidates based on their qualifications, experience, and personalties

    3. Interview and negotiation

    We will arrange the qualified talents for an interview with employers. We will follow up with the successful candidates for negotiation until get on board.
